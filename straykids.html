About Stray Kids

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment in 2017. The group consists of eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Felix, Han, Seungmin, and I.N. They are known for their energetic performances and self-produced music.

Stray Kids' Discography

Stray Kids has released several albums and singles since their debut. Some of their popular songs include "God's Menu," "Back Door," and "Miroh." They have also gained international recognition for their unique sound and powerful performances.

Stray Kids' Achievements

Stray Kids has a strong fanbase known as "STAY" and continues to grow in popularity both in South Korea and internationally. They are known for their energetic performances, self-produced music, and meaningful lyrics that resonate with their fans.

Stray Kids has received numerous awards and nominations for their music and performances. They have won several awards at the Mnet Asian Music Awards, Golden Disc Awards, and Seoul Music Awards, among others. The group has also been recognized for their influence on the K-pop industry and their global impact.
